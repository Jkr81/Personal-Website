About Me

I'm a game and immersive media developer at the University of Miami exploring strong interests in interactive storytelling, virtual reality, and experimental digital experiences.

My passion often lies in design, whether through user experience or player agency; my work thrives through systems-based thinking. My work moves between computer games and XR experiences, where I experiment with interaction, space, and storytelling using modern game engines and web-based tools.

Outside of interactive work, you'll often find me on set shooting or editing my next short film, or traveling in hopes of seeing one of the Seven Wonders of the world.
